      Common questions

      Frequently asked questions.

      Who can seek income tax filing assistance?

      Salaried individuals, pensioners, professionals, proprietors, businesses and eligible entities can share their filing requirement for review.

      Can you assist with multiple sources of income?

      Yes. Share records for salary, professional or business income, house property, capital gains, interest and other applicable sources.

      Can accounting work be handled with the return?

      Yes. Where filing depends on updated books, bank reconciliation, ledgers or financial statements, accounting assistance can be included.

      Do you also assist with GST and trademarks?

      Yes. GST registration and return assistance, along with trademark application support, are available as connected services.
