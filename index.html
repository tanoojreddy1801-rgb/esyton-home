FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Esyton provide?

Esyton offers non-assurance administrative and compliance support. We assist clients with filing Income Tax Returns, registering and forming new companies or LLPs, preparing monthly GST statements, handling routine business bookkeeping, filing trademark applications, and managing employee payroll files.

Can you help set up a new Private Limited Company or LLP from scratch?

Yes. We assist with the end-to-end process including checking name availability, acquiring Digital Signature Certificates (DSC), drafting Memorandum & Articles of Association (MoA/AoA) or Partnership Agreements, and submitting incorporation forms online.

How do you handle complex tax filings like stock market trading or FnO?

We systematically process and compile stock trading ledger data from major broker portals. Short-term, long-term, intraday, and Futures & Options (FnO) contract trades are categorized according to Income Tax guidelines to accurately determine your net taxable income or carry-forward losses.

Is my financial and business data kept secure?

Absolutely. All documentation shared with us is handled under strict confidentiality protocols and encrypted cloud storage systems. Your information is strictly used for preparing your requested filings.
