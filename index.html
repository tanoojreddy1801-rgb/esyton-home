Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about filing assistance and connected services.

Who can seek income tax filing assistance?

Salaried individuals, pensioners, professionals, proprietors, businesses and eligible entities can share their filing requirement for review.

Can you assist when I have multiple sources of income?

Yes. Share details and supporting records for salary, professional or business income, house property, capital gains, interest and other applicable sources.

Can accounting work be handled along with the return?

Yes. Where filing depends on updated books, bank reconciliation, ledgers or financial statements, accounting assistance can be included in the scope.

Do you also assist with GST and trademarks?

Yes. GST registration and return-related assistance, as well as trademark application support, are available as connected services.

What should I include in my first email?

Mention your taxpayer type, financial year, main income sources, the service required and any deadline or notice involved.
