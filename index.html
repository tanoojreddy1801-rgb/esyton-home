      Common questions

      Frequently asked questions.

      Who can seek income tax filing assistance from Esyton?

      Salaried individuals, pensioners, professionals, proprietors, businesses and eligible entities can share their filing requirement for review. We support a wide range of taxpayer profiles across India.

      Can you assist with returns involving multiple income sources?

      Yes. Share records for salary, professional or business income, house property, capital gains, interest, dividend and any other applicable source. We review all available information before proceeding.

      Can accounting work be handled alongside the return?

      Yes. Where your filing depends on updated books, bank reconciliation, ledgers or financial statements, accounting assistance can be included as part of the engagement.

      Do you assist with GST registration and returns?

      Yes. GST registration, amendment and periodic return assistance are available. We also help reconcile sales, purchase and ITC data for accurate compliance.

      What is your trademark application assistance scope?

      We support the organisation of applicant and brand details, class selection and documentation preparation for trademark applications. We also assist with follow-up and correspondence.
