FAQ Help Desk

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Esyton offer online?

Esyton is a digital business services firm that manages non-assurance administrative tasks. We assist clients with structuring their tax return drafts, sorting accounting income files, generating GST logs, organizing corporate payroll calculations, and assembling trademark applications. We do not issue formal public audit certificates or attest papers.

How does the platform handle share market trading losses like FnO?

Our filing support systems check and organize stock trading statements against the rules of the Income Tax Act. Speculative daily trading losses (intraday equity records) are neatly separated into individual storage boxes so they can be filed correctly. General capital market items (like Futures & Options contracts) are evaluated through clear matching routines to arrive at your true net figures before the return forms are drafted.

Can I link my regular business books directly with my monthly tax filings?

Yes. Esyton syncs transactional bookkeeping files directly with periodic return frameworks. Reconciled cash ledger databases, accounts receivable summaries, and inventory logs flow into tax filing sheets, helping to minimize variances before final returns are formatted for portal processing.

What information is needed to initialize an online trademark search request?

To initialize an identity packet, you provide applicant entity parameters (such as Individual, Startup, or Private Limited structures), design files or brand word vectors, and a detailed description of your core commercial operations. We map these inputs to appropriate international Nice Classification tracks and scan public registries to check for conflicting markers early.
